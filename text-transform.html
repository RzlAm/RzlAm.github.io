Text Transform

Transform your text into uppercase, lowercase, capitalize, initial and inherit.

How To Use

  • Enter your text in text area
  • Click the button uppercase, lowercase, capitalize or inherit.
  • You can copy text by clicking the copy button

Type Of Text Transform

1. Uppercase

Make all text to uppercase.

1. Lowercase

Make all text to lowercase.

1. Capitalize

Make fist word of every text to uppercase.

1. Inherit

Default option.
